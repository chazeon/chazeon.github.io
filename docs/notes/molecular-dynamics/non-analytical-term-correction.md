Perform [a seperate VASP run](https://phonopy.github.io/phonopy/vasp.html#non-analytical-term-correction-optional) to calculate the *Born effective charge* and *dielectric constant*, then use [`phonopy-vasp-born`](https://phonopy.github.io/phonopy/auxiliary-tools.html#phonopy-vasp-born) to post-process.
